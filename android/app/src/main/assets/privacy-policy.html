Privacy Policy

for MyActivityMonitor
Last updated: October 22, 2025

Overview

MyActivityMonitor is a simple application that shows your daily activity using data available on your device. We designed the app to read the minimum necessary information and to process it on the device.

Health Connect data we access (Android)

On Android devices that support Health Connect, the app requests read-only access to the following record types, solely to display your daily progress inside the app:

Data type Purpose Scope
StepsRecord (Steps) Calculate and display the total number of steps for the current day. Read-only; aggregated on-device for “today”.
ExerciseSessionRecord (Exercise sessions) Calculate and display total active minutes for the current day. Read-only; aggregated on-device for “today”.

The app does not request write access and does not access any other Health Connect data types.

How we use your information

  • To compute and display your steps and active minutes for the day.
  • To compare those values against goals that you set in the app.

We do not use your health data for advertising, profiling, or analytics.

On‑device processing & data sharing

Health data read through Health Connect is processed on your device only. We do not transfer, sell, or share your health data with third parties. The app does not maintain remote servers for health data.

Permissions & control

  • You can grant or revoke Health Connect permissions at any time in the system settings.
  • If permissions are revoked, the app will stop reading those data types and will display zeros or placeholders.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time; the app does not export your health data off-device.

Other data

The app may store your goal settings (e.g., steps and minutes targets) locally on the device so they persist across app launches. These settings do not contain health data and are not shared.

Data retention

MyActivityMonitor does not retain Health Connect records. Health data is queried from Health Connect when you open or refresh the app and is not stored by the app. Locally stored goal settings remain until you change them or uninstall the app.

Data deletion

MyActivityMonitor does not collect or store any personal or health data outside your device. All data used by the app — such as your daily steps and activity sessions — are read directly from Health Connect and remain under your control within that system.

You can delete your data at any time in one of the following ways:

  • By uninstalling the app, which removes all locally stored goal settings and preferences from your device.
  • By using the Health Connect system app to delete the underlying health records (e.g., steps or exercise sessions).

Because the app does not store data on external servers, no additional deletion request is required.

Security

We rely on the platform security of Android and Health Connect to control access to health data. The app requests the minimum permissions required and uses read-only access.

Children’s privacy

MyActivityMonitor is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided health data through the app, please revoke Health Connect permissions and uninstall the app.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above.

Contact
